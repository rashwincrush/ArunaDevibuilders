Concrete Methods — The Right Mix for the Right Job

On-site measuring-box batching + RMC when needed.

What we do

  • On-site measuring-box batching for small/medium pours to maintain proportion control, avoid pump MOQs/premiums, and reduce cold‑joint risks in tight urban sites.
  • RMC for large continuous pours (slabs/basements) for speed and consistency, with on‑site QA checks.
  • QA & documentation: mixer calibration log (periodic), batch diary, slump window & placement timings, cube tests, curing documentation, waterproofing proofs.

On‑site mixer: maximum proportion control; best for phased pours. RMC: best for uninterrupted, long pours. We maintain daily WhatsApp updates, CCTV till completion, and a 20‑Year Structural Warranty & Workmanship Guarantee across our delivery.
