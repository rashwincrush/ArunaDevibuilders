Materials & Brands

Brand selections are finalized in the BOQ with your approval. QA logs are recorded for each category.

Batch records are maintained for cement type, grade, and mix design. Concrete pours are logged with date, location, and curing plan. Slump tests and cube tests (where applicable) are recorded for QA.

Heat numbers, diameters, and IS codes are recorded for rebar. Mill test certificates and site inspection notes are archived for traceability.

Brick/block type and density are documented. Delivery receipts and on-site QA (dimensions, soundness) are noted before use.

Cable gauge, switchgear ratings, and device schedules are recorded in BOQ. Test reports (continuity/insulation) are logged during commissioning.

Pipe materials/sizes and valve specs are documented. Pressure tests and fixture checks are recorded prior to handover.

System type, surface prep, and application logs are maintained. Ponding tests and manufacturer guidelines are referenced for QA.

Tile make/sizes, grout types, and paint systems are recorded. Room-wise finish schedules and approvals are part of the BOQ set.

Door/window specs (material, size, hardware) are documented. Manufacturer warranties and installation checklists are filed.

Sanitary, lighting, and accessory models are recorded with brand, finish, and warranty details. Final fit-off checks are logged.
