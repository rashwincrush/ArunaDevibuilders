Technology & Methods

Precision Engineering Methods

We employ the latest technology and proven methodologies to ensure structural integrity and quality.

Concrete Methodology

Our concrete work follows strict quality control measures:

  • Measuring-box system for accurate aggregate proportions
  • Cement measurement by bag count for consistency
  • Regular mixer calibration and batch diary maintenance
  • Slump testing and cube testing for strength verification
  • Proper curing procedures for optimal strength development

For large pours, we prefer RMC (Ready Mix Concrete) to ensure uninterrupted placement and consistent quality.

Quality Testing Protocols

Our comprehensive QA process includes:

  • Daily site inspections and progress documentation
  • Material testing at certified laboratories
  • Waterproofing tests for terraces and wet areas
  • Alignment checks at every structural stage
  • Electrical and plumbing system testing before closing
  • Final leak tests and functional verification

All test results are documented and provided to clients during handover.
