Architectural Design — Concept to GFC

Lifestyle‑fit layouts that meet codes and approvals.

What we do

  • Concept → sanction‑ready GFC drawings (floor plans, elevations, sections, schedules).
  • Approvals documentation and compliance mapping for CMDA/DTCP (zoning, setbacks, FSI, height).
  • Lifestyle‑fit planning: circulation, natural light/ventilation, storage, and elevation character.
  • Finishes and joinery coordination within turnkey scope to avoid rework.

GFC (Good‑for‑Construction) drawings are the final issued set used on site. They lock dimensions and details after design iterations and approvals, minimizing ambiguity during execution.
