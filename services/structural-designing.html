Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our structural design services.

What IS codes do you follow in your structural designs?

We follow all relevant Indian Standard codes including IS 456 (Concrete), IS 800 (Steel), IS 1893 (Seismic), IS 875 (Wind loads), IS 13920 (Ductile detailing), and other applicable codes based on project requirements.

Do you provide structural design for renovation projects?

Yes, we provide structural design services for renovation, retrofitting, and extension projects. This includes structural assessment of existing buildings, design of new structural elements, and integration with existing structures.

How long does the structural design process typically take?

The timeline depends on project complexity and size. For a typical residential project, the structural design process takes 2-4 weeks. Commercial and larger projects may require 4-8 weeks. We provide a detailed project schedule during the initial consultation.

What software do you use for structural analysis and design?

We use industry-standard software including ETABS, STAAD.Pro, SAFE, and manual calculations as required. For complex projects, we may use specialized software for dynamic analysis, soil-structure interaction, or other advanced analyses.

Is the structural warranty transferable if I sell the property?

Yes, our 20-year structural warranty is transferable to subsequent property owners, which can be a valuable selling point. The warranty terms and transfer process are detailed in the warranty documentation provided upon project completion.
