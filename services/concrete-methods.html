Our Concrete Methodology

We employ a dual-approach concrete methodology that combines the precision of on-site batching with the efficiency of ready-mix concrete, tailored to each project's specific requirements.

On-site Measuring-Box Batching

For small to medium pours where precision control is paramount, we use calibrated measuring boxes to ensure exact proportion control.

  • Avoids pump MOQs and premiums
  • Reduces cold-joint risks in tight urban sites
  • Ideal for phased construction projects
  • Maximum proportion control and flexibility

Ready-Mix Concrete (RMC)

For large continuous pours requiring speed and consistency, we utilize quality-assured ready-mix concrete with on-site QA checks.

  • Best for uninterrupted, long pours
  • Consistent quality and mix proportions
  • Ideal for slabs, basements, and large foundations
  • Time-efficient for large-scale projects

Quality Assurance & Documentation

Our comprehensive QA process ensures every concrete pour meets the highest standards.

Mixer Calibration Logs

Periodic calibration of mixing equipment to ensure consistent proportions and quality.

Batch Diary & Records

Detailed documentation of each batch including mix proportions, timings, and conditions.

Slump Testing

Regular slump tests to ensure proper workability and consistency of the concrete mix.

Cube Testing

Compressive strength testing of concrete cubes to verify design strength requirements.

Curing Documentation

Systematic recording of curing methods, duration, and environmental conditions.

Waterproofing Proofs

Verification and documentation of waterproofing applications and effectiveness.
