Why Our Method is Better

Measured / On-site Concrete
Our Method
  • Precise batching with measuring boxes for aggregates & M‑sand
  • Immediate on-site control, supervised mixes, documented checks
  • Reduced risk of delays/variations; consistency you can verify
RMC‑only Approach
Typical
  • Less direct control at site; risk of transit delays & mix variation
  • Dependency on vendor schedules and quality disclosures
  • Harder to log/verify batch‑wise quality at the pour
