Engineering & Process

Scientific methods from first peg to final handover.

6-Step Flow

Survey
Total‑station surveying & site analysis
Soil Testing
Comprehensive investigation (included)
BOQ
Detailed bill of quantities & schedule
RCC Work
On‑site mixer + measuring boxes / RMC (large pours)
QA Testing
Daily QA checks, waterproofing proofs, CCTV refs
Handover
Final inspection, T&C, documents & warranty

Process details

  • Digital land survey + total‑station marking for accurate grids and levels.
  • Compulsory soil testing (included; no extra charge).
  • BOQ/quantities via calibrated measuring‑box for aggregates & M‑sand; cement by bag count.
  • Mixer calibration log (periodic) and batch diary for pours.
  • Testing & documentation: slump window & placement timings, cube tests, curing records, wet‑area/terrace waterproofing proofs.
  • Visibility: daily WhatsApp digest and CCTV till completion.
  • Handover checks: leak tests, alignment, electrical/plumbing verification, signed handover.

Measuring‑box keeps on‑site proportion control; RMC is preferred for uninterrupted, large pours where speed and consistency matter.
