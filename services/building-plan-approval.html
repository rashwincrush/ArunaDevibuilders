Our Approval Process

We follow a systematic approach to ensure your building plan approval is processed efficiently

Initial Consultation & Document Collection

We begin by understanding your project requirements and collecting all necessary documents including land ownership papers, existing structure details, and project specifications.

Site Analysis & Feasibility Study

Our team conducts a thorough site analysis to assess zoning regulations, land use classification, and other local bylaws that may impact your project.

Architectural & Structural Drawing Preparation

We prepare detailed architectural and structural drawings that comply with all regulatory requirements, including FSI calculations, setback provisions, and safety norms.

Application Submission & Tracking

We submit the complete application package to the relevant authorities and provide regular updates on the application status throughout the approval process.

Objection Handling & Resubmission

If any objections are raised, we address them promptly with the necessary modifications and resubmit the application for approval.

Final Approval & Documentation

Once approved, we ensure you receive all necessary documentation and provide guidance on next steps for your construction project.
