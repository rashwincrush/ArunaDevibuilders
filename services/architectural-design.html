Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your architectural design service?

Our comprehensive service includes initial consultation, concept development, detailed drawings (floor plans, elevations, sections), 3D visualizations, material specifications, and GFC (Good for Construction) drawings. We also assist with approval documentation for local authorities.

How long does the architectural design process take?

The timeline varies based on project complexity, but typically takes 4-8 weeks from initial consultation to final GFC drawings. This includes time for client feedback and revisions at each stage.

Do you handle building plan approvals?

Yes, we prepare all necessary documentation for CMDA/DTCP approvals and can guide you through the submission process. However, the actual submission is typically done by the client or their authorized representative.

Can you work with my existing architect's plans?

Absolutely. We can review, modify, or enhance existing plans to better suit your needs or ensure compliance with current regulations.

What information do I need to provide to get started?

We'll need your site details (survey plan, dimensions, orientation), your requirements (number of rooms, specific needs, budget), and any inspiration images or ideas you have. We guide you through this process during our initial consultation.
