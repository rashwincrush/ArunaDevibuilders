Our Turnkey Construction Process

A systematic approach to ensure quality at every stage of your dream home construction

1. Consultation & Site Analysis

We begin with understanding your requirements, budget, and site conditions including soil testing and survey.

2. Design & BOQ Preparation

Our architects create customized designs while our engineers prepare detailed BOQ with transparent costing.

3. Approvals & Permits

We handle all necessary building plan approvals and permits from local authorities.

4. Foundation & Structure

Quality-controlled RCC work with proper basement planning up to 7 ft with waterproofing solutions.

5. MEP & Finishes

Integration of electrical, plumbing, and HVAC systems followed by premium finishes of your choice.

6. Quality Assurance

Rigorous quality checks including concrete testing, waterproofing validation, and finishing inspections.

7. Final Handover

Complete documentation, warranty details, and keys handed over with a thorough walkthrough.

8. Post-Handover Support

Continued support and maintenance services as per our warranty terms.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our turnkey construction process

What is included in your turnkey package?

Our turnkey package includes everything from architectural design, structural engineering, approvals, construction, materials, finishes, and final handover. We provide a detailed BOQ so you know exactly what's included.

Do you provide a warranty for your construction?

Yes, we provide a comprehensive 20-year structural warranty along with a workmanship guarantee. This covers any defects in construction quality or materials.

How do you ensure quality during construction?

We implement rigorous quality control measures including concrete testing, material verification, waterproofing checks, and daily supervision. You also receive daily progress updates with photos.

What is your payment structure?

For projects over 2,000 sq.ft, we offer a zero-advance option with progress-linked payments. Alternatively, you can choose a 10% advance with stage-wise payment schedule. All payment terms are transparently outlined in our agreement.

How long does a typical turnkey project take?

The timeline depends on the size and complexity of the project. A standard 3,000 sq.ft home typically takes 8-10 months from design to handover. We provide a detailed project schedule during the planning phase.
